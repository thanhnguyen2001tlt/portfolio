The Pioneers Behind the Phenomenon: But Tre and the Rise of Thế Giới Game.
View Project

In the bustling realm of gaming media, there existed a remarkable team known as But Tre, whose unwavering dedication and relentless pursuit of excellence birthed a groundbreaking publication: Thế Giới Game (World of Game). For years, we served as the driving force behind this magazine, meticulously curating and managing its content, captivating the hearts of countless young Vietnamese gamers. Under my steadfast leadership, Thế Giới Game swiftly ascended to become the ultimate destination for gaming enthusiasts across the nation, reigning supreme until VIETGAME entered the market, ready to claim the throne. Together, as a harmonious collective of visionaries and passionate gamers, we breathed life into the pages of Thế Giới Game. Our shared mission was to immerse readers in a vibrant world where gaming was not just a pastime but a way of life. With each meticulously crafted article, insightful review, and captivating feature, we aimed to ignite the imaginations of our audience, introducing them to the latest trends, game releases, and insider secrets of the gaming realm. Thế Giới Game quickly became the epicenter of gaming culture, an indispensable companion for enthusiasts seeking to stay at the forefront of the industry. Through our tireless efforts and unyielding commitment to excellence, we fostered a loyal community that eagerly awaited each issue, devouring its contents with insatiable enthusiasm. For a glorious chapter in time, Thế Giới Game held the throne as the most beloved gaming magazine among the vibrant youth of Vietnam. However, as with all tales of triumph, new challengers emerged on the horizon. VIETGAME, armed with its own unique vision and unyielding passion, ventured into the gaming media landscape, poised to captivate the hearts of gamers nationwide. With their entry, the crown that once adorned Thế Giới Game’s head was relinquished, signaling a new era in the industry. Thế Giới Game may have graced the shelves of the past, but its impact endures, an indelible testament to the power of passion, collaboration, and the unwavering bond between gaming and the human spirit.
