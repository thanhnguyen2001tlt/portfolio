Rising Empire

Pioneering the Web Gaming Revolution Back in 2009, amidst a gaming landscape dominated by traditional MMORPGs and casual titles, I embarked on a bold venture that would forever reshape the industry. With unwavering determination, I introduced Rising Empire, a groundbreaking web-based game that defied conventions and set the stage for a digital revolution.

View More

Breaking free from the constraints of installations and extensive screen time, Rising Empire offered a seamless experience directly through web browsers. It was a game-changer, capturing the hearts and minds of players across Vietnam and beyond. Adults and white-collar workers, in particular, found solace in its immersive world, and soon enough, Rising Empire became the undisputed market leader. During its peak, the game boasted an average of 500,000 concurrent users—an astounding feat that showcased its magnetic appeal. The impact was undeniable, sparking a wave of web games throughout Vietnam and beyond. Rising Empire became a symbol of innovation and a catalyst for change, forever leaving its mark on the gaming landscape.
